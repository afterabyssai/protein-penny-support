Account Deletion - Protein Penny

How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Protein Penny account and all associated data:

Email: support@afterabyss.com
Subject: Delete My Account - Protein Penny
Include: Your registered email address

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user account and profile settings
  • All daily food logs
  • Custom foods you created
  • Saved favorites
  • All personal preferences and data

Timeline

Account deletions are processed within 30 days of your request.

Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
